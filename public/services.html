Primary care:

    General Check-ups and Preventive Care: Regular health check-ups, vaccinations, screenings, and health assessments.
    Chronic Disease Management: Ongoing care for conditions like diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and heart disease.
    Health Risk Assessments: Evaluating lifestyle and family history to identify potential health risks.

Specialised care:

    Pediatrics: Healthcare services for children, including vaccinations, growth monitoring, and development assessments.
    Geriatrics: Care specifically tailored to elderly patients, including managing age-related conditions like dementia and mobility issues.
    Women's Health: Gynecological exams, family planning, prenatal care, menopause management, and screenings for breast and cervical cancers.
    Men's Health: Prostate screenings, sexual health, testosterone therapy, and other age-related concerns.
    Mental Health Services: Counseling, therapy, psychiatric evaluations, and support for mental health conditions such as depression, anxiety, and stress management.
    Dermatology: Skin care services, including mole checks, acne treatment, and cosmetic dermatology.
    Cardiology: Diagnosis and treatment of heart conditions, including ECG, stress tests, and heart disease management.
    Orthopedics: Diagnosis and treatment of bone, joint, and muscle issues, including fractures, arthritis, and sports injuries.
    Endocrinology: Management of hormone imbalances, thyroid conditions, and diabetes care.

3.Diagnostic Services

    Laboratory Services: Blood tests, urine tests, and other diagnostic exams.
    Imaging Services: X-rays, MRIs, CT scans, ultrasound, and other imaging technologies.
    Genetic Testing: Testing for inherited conditions and understanding genetic predispositions.

4.Emergency and Urgent Care

    Emergency Care: Immediate treatment for accidents, injuries, and life-threatening conditions.
    Urgent Care: For non-life-threatening conditions that require prompt attention, such as colds, flu, minor injuries, and infections.

5.Surgical Services

    Minor Surgeries: Outpatient procedures such as mole removal, biopsies, or stitch work.
    Major Surgeries: More complex procedures requiring inpatient care, like orthopedic surgeries, appendectomies, or cardiac surgeries.
    Minimally Invasive Surgery: Laparoscopy, endoscopy, and other minimally invasive techniques.

6.Rehabilitation Services

    Rehabilitation for musculoskeletal injuries, recovery post-surgery, or pain management.
    Occupational Therapy: Help with regaining skills needed for daily activities following injury, illness, or surgery.
    Speech Therapy: Treatment for speech, language, and swallowing disorders.

7.Palliative and Hospice Care

    Palliative Care: Symptom management for chronic or terminal illnesses, focused on comfort and quality of life.
    Hospice Care: End-of-life care with emotional, spiritual, and physical support for both patients and their families.

8.Health and Wellness Programs

    Nutrition Counseling: Guidance on healthy eating habits, weight management, and dietary needs.
    Fitness Programs: Exercise classes, personalized fitness plans, and rehabilitation services.
    Smoking Cessation: Support and resources to help patients quit smoking.

9.Telemedicine and Online Services

    Virtual Consultations: Online appointments with doctors or specialists for consultations, follow-ups, and non-emergency care.
    Online Prescriptions: Ability to receive prescriptions via telemedicine consultations or online services.

10.Pharmacy Services

    Prescription Fulfillment: Online pharmacy services or on-site pharmacy to fill prescriptions.
    Medication Management: Counseling on proper medication use, possible interactions, and alternatives.

11.Cosmetic and Aesthetic Services

    Skin Care Treatments: Botox, dermal fillers, chemical peels, and other skin rejuvenation treatments.
    Laser Treatments: Hair removal, tattoo removal, or skin resurfacing services.

12.Patient Support Services:

    Care Coordination: Help navigating complex healthcare needs, managing referrals, and organizing treatments.
    Patient Education: Access to health information, disease prevention tips, and educational resources.
    Insurance Assistance: Help understanding insurance benefits, billing, and payment plans.
